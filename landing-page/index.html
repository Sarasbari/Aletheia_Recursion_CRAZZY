why this exists

Why ALETHEIA

Cryptographic guarantees at every layer — from pixel to proof.

Tamper-proof Hashing

Dual-hash approach: SHA-512 for bit-level integrity, pHash for perceptual similarity. Change a single pixel and the math breaks.

SHA-512 + pHash → dual verification

Decentralized Storage

IPFS means no single server owns your proof. Content-addressed, censorship-resistant, globally accessible. Your data outlives us.

IPFS CID → permanent retrieval

On-chain Proof

Merkle tree batching on Polygon. Immutable timestamps anyone can verify, forever, for fractions of a cent.

Polygon tx → immutable timestamp
